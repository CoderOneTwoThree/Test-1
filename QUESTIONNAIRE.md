# Workout Plan Intake Questionnaire

## Goals
- What is your primary fitness goal?
- Do you have a secondary goal?
- What is your target timeline for this plan?

## Experience & Current Activity
- How long have you been training consistently?
- What types of training have you done before?
- How many days per week are you currently active?

## Schedule & Availability
- How many days per week can you train?
- How much time can you spend per session?
- Which days of the week are you available to train?

## Equipment & Environment
- What equipment do you have access to?
- Where will you train (home, gym, outdoors)?

## Health & Limitations
- Do you have any injuries, pain, or medical conditions to consider?
- Are there any exercises or movements you must avoid?

## Preferences
- What types of training do you enjoy?
- What types of training do you dislike or want to avoid?
- Do you prefer full-body or split routines?
- Do you want cardio included? If yes, what kind?

## Measurements & Constraints
- What is your age?
- What is your sex?
- What is your height?
- What is your current body weight?
